Budgeting Help

Budgeting your money can be a very difficult thing. How much do you have to spend monthly? What if you want to save a certian amount of money? Just enter a few things about yourself, and we can make the calculations for you, and hopefully make your life a little bit easier!

Info

  • Salary: Enter the amount of money you make in a year
  • Amount spent per month: Enter an estimate of your monthly spending. (Bills, Gas, Rent, etc)
  • This will calculate the money that you have leftover, after all expenses are paid
